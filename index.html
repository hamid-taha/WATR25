Workshop on Advances in Telecommunications Research (WATR)

June 14-16, 2025 | Queen’s University, Kingston, Ontario

Scope

Join us at WATR 2025, where leading researchers, industry experts, and practitioners will explore groundbreaking advancements in telecommunications. This workshop provides a dynamic platform for exchanging ideas, fostering collaborations, and shaping the future of telecommunications networks. This year’s theme focuses on interdisciplinary innovations shaping the next generation of telecommunications. Key discussions will explore how AI, quantum computing, and other emerging technologies are transforming the field.

Workshop Format

WATR 2025 will follow a structured daily module format, ensuring a balanced mix of presentations, discussions, and actionable insights. Each day will center around a specific theme.

Day 1: AI and Reasoning in the Network

Exploring the role of artificial intelligence in autonomous decision-making, network optimization, and reasoning frameworks in telecommunications.

Day 2: Quantum Communications and Computing

Investigating the intersection of quantum technologies with telecommunications, including quantum key distribution, quantum networking, and the impact of quantum computing on secure and high-performance communication systems.

Day 3: Transformative Applications and Services

Exploring the latest advancements in telecommunications applications and service models, including new paradigms in sensing, connectivity, and network infrastructure.
