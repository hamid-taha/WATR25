Workshop on Advances in Telecommunications Research (WATR)

June 16-18, 2025 | Queen’s University, Kingston, Ontario

Generously supported by the IEEE Sections in Kingston and Waterloo-Kitchener.

Scope

Join us at WATR 2025, where leading researchers, industry experts, and practitioners will explore groundbreaking advancements in telecommunications. This workshop provides a dynamic platform for exchanging ideas, fostering collaborations, and shaping the future of telecommunications networks. This year’s theme focuses on interdisciplinary innovations shaping the next generation of telecommunications. Key discussions will explore how AI, quantum computing, and other emerging technologies are transforming the field.

Workshop Program

Download Full Program (PDF)

The three-day WATR 2025 workshop is structured around key themes that reflect the evolving landscape of telecommunications research:

  • Day 1 – AI and 6G: Exploring native intelligence, large language models, and terahertz communications.
  • Day 2 – The Transformative Network: Quantum systems, edge computing, and resilient networking solutions.
  • Day 3 – Advances in IoT: Tactile internet, biomedical sensing, and human-centered IoT applications.
